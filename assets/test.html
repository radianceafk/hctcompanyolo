Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our shipping and logistics services

What documents are required for shipping from Dubai?

Typically, you'll need a commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, and bill of lading. Specific requirements vary by destination country and commodity type. Our team will provide you with a complete checklist based on your shipment details.

How long does shipping from Dubai to Europe typically take?

Transit times vary by service level: Economy sea freight takes 20-30 days, Standard air freight takes 7-10 days, and Express air freight takes 2-4 days. These are door-to-door estimates including customs clearance at destination.

Do you handle customs clearance in destination countries?

Yes, we offer comprehensive customs brokerage services in all countries we serve. Our network of local experts ensures smooth clearance and compliance with import regulations. We manage all documentation, duties, and taxes on your behalf.

What types of cargo do you specialize in?

We handle general merchandise, perishables, hazardous materials, high-value goods, oversized equipment, and temperature-sensitive products. Our team has specific expertise in electronics, textiles, automotive parts, medical supplies, and retail goods.

How do you ensure the security of high-value shipments?

We employ multiple security measures including GPS tracking, tamper-evident seals, dedicated transport, and secure storage facilities. For high-value shipments, we offer enhanced insurance options and specialized handling protocols.
